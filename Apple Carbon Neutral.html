    P r o j e c t

    Apple have pledged to make their products carbon neutral by 2030. To achieve this goal, they will reduce their 2015 emissions (38.4 million metric tons CO2e) by 75% by 2030. The remaining 25% of gross emissions (9.6 million metric tons CO2e) will be removed using carbon offsets, bringing the net emissions to 0.

    I will be using the data provided by Apple in their Environmental Progress Reports to visualize their progress towards becoming carbon neutral in 2030.



    D a s h b o a r d

    I built this dashboard using Power BI. I used line charts and a bar graph to clearly display whether Apple will be carbon neutral by 2030. I used a line chart as it is an effective way of displaying changes over time, where I could clearly present Apple's emissions data each year. Using a line chart also enabled me to utilise Power BI's forecast feature to show predictions based on Apple's past and present data. Forecasting is important as it can play a big role in the company's future planning, affecting their future descisions and resource allocation. I used a basic colour scheme, using purple and grey to keep the focus on the data, with hits of gold to showcase Apple's target emissions.



    What I Learnt:

    • How to use Power BI's forecasting capability to predict future results based on your current data.

    • How to use an effective colour scheme that highlights the data you want to show.
