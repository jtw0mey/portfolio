    Apple Carbon Neutral Dashboard



    P r o j e c t
    Apple have pledged to make their products carbon neutral by 2030. To achieve this goal, they will reduce their 2015 emissions (38.4 million metric tons CO2e) by 75% by 2030. The remaining 25% of gross emissions (9.6 million metric tons CO2e) will be removed using carbon offsets, bringing the net emissions to 0.
    I will be using the data provided by Apple in their Environmental Progress Reports to visualize their progress towards becoming carbon neutral in 2030.

    D a s h b o a r d
    I built this dashboard using Power BI. I used line charts and a bar graph to clearly display whether Apple will be carbon neutral by 2030. I used line charts as they are great for displaying changes over time. I was also able to utilise Power BI's forecast feature to show predictions based on Apple's past and present data. I used a basic colour scheme, using purple and grey to keep the focus on the data, with hits of gold to showcase Apple's target emissions.
