    Steve's Car Showroom


    P r o j e c t

    Steve runs a car showroom and his data analyst has quit leaving him without crucial insights. I plan to analyze his data providing him with answers to the following questions:

    1. What are the details of all cars purchased in the year 2022?
    2. What is the total number of cars sold by each salesperson?
    3. What is the total revenue generated by each salesperson?
    4. What are the details of the cars sold by each salesperson?
    5. What is the total revenue generated by each car type?
    6. What are the details of the cars sold in the year 2021 by salesperson 'Emily Wong'?
    7. What is the total revenue generated by the sales of hatchback cars?
    8. What is the total revenue generated by the sales of SUV cars in the year 2022?
    9. What is the name and city of the salesperson who sold the most number of cars in the year 2023?
    10. What is the name and age of the salesperson who generated the highest revenue in the year 2022?


    D a t a s e t

    Three tables have been created for Sales, Cars and Salespersons. I will be using this data to provide answers to Steve's questions.




    1. What are the details of all cars purchased in the year 2022?


    I selected all the columns from the Cars table. I then joined the Sales tables with the Cars table using the mutual car_id  column to be able to access the purchase date of each sale. I then used where to specify cars purchased in 2022 only.








    2. What is the total number of cars sold by each salesperson?


    The total number of cars sold by each salesperson was calculated by counting the number of sale_id's from the Sales table and joining it with the corresponding name from the Salesperson's table using the mutual salesman_id  column.








    3. What is the total revenue generated by each salesperson?


    The total revenue by each salesperson was calculated by summing the cost of each car the salesperson sold by joining the Sales table and Cars table with the mutual car_id  column. The salesperson's names was also selected by joining the Sales table and Salespersons table using the mutual salesman_id  column.








    4. What are the details of the cars sold by each salesperson?


    To show the details of the cars sold by each salesperson I selected columns from all three tables. This was done by joining the Sales table and Salespersons table using the mutual salesman_id  and joining the Cars table and Sales table using the mutual car_id  column.








    5. What is the total revenue generated by each car type?


    The total revenue generated by each car type was calculated by summing the cost of each car type by how many times it was sold by joining the Sales table and Cars table using the mutual car_id  column.








    6. What are the details of the cars sold in the year 2021 by salesperson 'Emily Wong'?


    I displayed the details of the cars sold by Emily Wong by joining all 3 tables using the mutual car_id  and salesman_id  columns. I then selected the columns that I wanted to show before using a where clause to specify only cars sold by Emily Wong in 2021.








    7. What is the total revenue generated by the sales of hatchback cars?


    Hatchback cars generated $100,000 revenue.

    I calculated the total revenue generated by hatchback cars by summing the cost of each car by how many times it was sold by joining the Cars table and Sales table using the mutual car_id  column. I then used a where clause to specify hatchback cars only.








    8. What is the total revenue generated by the sales of SUV cars in the year 2022?


    SUV's generated $150,000 revenue in 2022.

    I used the same process as question 7, but I updated the where clause to show only SUV sales in 2022.








    9. What is the name and city of the salesperson who sold the most number of cars in the year 2023?


    Tom Lee from Seattle sold the most cars in 2023.

    I started by selecting the name and city columns from the Salespersons table. I then counted how many sales each person made by joining the Sales and Salespersons table using the mutual salesman_id  column. I then used a where clause to specify only sales in 2023.








    10. What is the name and age of the salesperson who generated the highest revenue in the year 2022?


    35 year old Emily Wong generated the highest revenue in 2022.

    I started by selecting the name and age columns from the Salespersons table. I then summed the cost of each car by how many times it was sold by joining the Sales table and Cars table using the mutual car_id  column. I also joined the Salespersons table and Sales table using the mutual salesman_id  column. I then used a where clause to specify sales in 2022 only.
