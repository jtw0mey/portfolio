    Tom's Car Showroom

    SQL



    P r o j e c t

    Tom runs a car showroom and his data analyst has just quit leaving him without crucial insights. I plan to analyze his data providing him with answers to the following questions:

    1. What are the details of all cars purchased in the year 2022?
    2. What is the total number of cars sold by each salesperson?
    3. What is the total revenue generated by each salesperson?
    4. What are the details of the cars sold by each salesperson?
    5. What is the total revenue generated by each car type?
    6. What are the details of the cars sold in the year 2021 by salesperson 'Emily Wong'?
    7. What is the total revenue generated by the sales of hatchback cars?
    8. What is the total revenue generated by the sales of SUV cars in the year 2022?
    9. What is the name and city of the salesperson who sold the most number of cars in the year 2023?
    10. What is the name and age of the salesperson who generated the highest revenue in the year 2022?


    D a t a s e t

    Three tables have been created for Sales, Cars and Salespersons. I will be using this data to provide answers to Tom's questions.





    1. What are the details of all cars purchased in the year 2022?


    I selected all the columns from the Cars table. I then used join to combine the columns from the Sales tables with the Cars table using the mutual car_id  column to show all the details for each car purchased. I then used where to specify the cars that were purchased in 2022 only.








    2. What is the total number of cars sold by each salesperson?


    The total number of cars sold by each salesperson was calculated by counting the number of sale_id's from the Sales table and joining it with the corresponding name from the Salesperson's table using the mutual salesman_id  column.








    3. What is the total revenue generated by each salesperson?


    The total revenue by each salesperson was calculated by joining the Sales table and the Cars table, we were then able to multiply the car's cost with the amount of cars each salesperson sold. The salesperson's names were used by joining the Sales table and Salespersons table using the mutual salesman_id  column.








    4. What are the details of the cars sold by each salesperson?


    To show the details of the cars sold by each salesperson I had to select columns from all three tables. This was done by joining the tables using the mutual salesman_id and car_id columns.








    5. What is the total revenue generated by each car type?


    The total revenue generated by each car type was calculated by joining the Cars and Sales table to be able to combine the car type's cost, with the amount of sales that car type had.








    6. What are the details of the cars sold in the year 2021 by salesperson 'Emily Wong'?


    I displayed the details of the cars sold by Emily Wong by joining all 3 tables using the mutual car_id  and salesman_id  columns. I then select the columns that I wanted to show before using a where clause to specify only cars sold by Emily Wong in 2021.








    7. What is the total revenue generated by the sales of hatchback cars?


    I calculated the total revenue generated by hatchback cars by joining the Cars and Sales table using the mutual car_id  column. I was then able to combine the car's style with how many times that style was sold. I then used a where clause to specify hatchback cars only.








    8. What is the total revenue generated by the sales of SUV cars in the year 2022?


    I used the same process as question 7, but I updated the where clause to show only SUV sales in 2022.








    9. What is the name and city of the salesperson who sold the most number of cars in the year 2023?


    To show the name and city of the salesperson with the most number of cars sold in 2023, I started by selecting the name and city columns from the Salespersons table. I then joined the Sales and Salespersons table using the mutual salesman_id  column. I was then able to count the sales for each person in 2023 specifically, using a where clause.








    10. What is the name and age of the salesperson who generated the highest revenue in the year 2022?


    To find out the name and age of the salesperson with the highest revenue in 2022 I started by selecting the name and age columns from the Salespersons table. To create the total revenue for 2022 column, I had to join all 3 tables using the salesman_id  and car_id  columns. I was then able to combine the car's cost with the sales data and salesperson's information to work out each salesperon's total revenue. I then used a where clause to specify sales in 2022 only. The table shows 35yr old Emily Wong had the highest revenue in 2022.
