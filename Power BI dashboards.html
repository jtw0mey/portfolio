    Gift World Sales Report


    P r o j e c t

    A fictional souvenir store, "Gifts World", have received their total sales data for the years 2018 - 2021. They have given me the task of producing a dashboard that clearly displays the quantity of sales, and what US states and category of store are producing the most sales. They also need to be able to filter between the years.



    D a s h b o a r d

    I built this dashboard using Power BI. My aim was to produce a modern, yet simple and clean dashboard that is engaging without loud colours to keep a professional feel - I chose a navy blue colour scheme for this. I used a bar graph for sales by category to clearly compare the different groups, and display which store type is producing the most sales. I used a line chart for the quantity of sales as it is a good vizualisation to track changes over time. I created a map to show which US states had the most sales using colour gradient, with individual states' sales data displaying when hovering over them.



    What I Learnt:

    • How to use 'positioning' in Power BI to ensure all images are perfectly aligned down to the exact pixel.

    • How to use an interactive map with gradient colour conditional formatting.

    • How to use a slider to filter between years which interacts with the Dashboard's data








    Data Survey Dashboard

    Dataset here

    P r o j e c t

    A fictional news website "Data WW", have conducted a survey of people who work in the data industry. They have given me the task to clean the data into usable formats, and create an aesthetic dashboard that displays the findings effectively.



    D a t a    C l e a n i n g

    The Excel file had a few columns that needed to be cleaned. I used the Excel formulas trim, split, and find & replace to clean and tranform the data that contained spelling mistakes and formatting differences. These formulas transformed the data in to a standardized format to be used in producing clean visualizations for the dashboard.



    D a s h b o a r d

    I built this dashboard using Power BI. I used a range of bar graphs, line charts, donut chats and gauge charts to produce a compelling visualization whilst displaying the results of the survey clearly and effectively. The colour scheme was made to match the colours of the new website, which is brown and earth tones.



    What I Learnt:

    • How to use gauge charts and stacked bar graph.

    • How to use bordering to fit multiple graphs in a small space without it looking clustered.
